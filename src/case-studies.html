E-Commerce Operations Automation

The Problem

A scaling e-commerce brand was spending 20+ hours/week manually updating inventory across Shopify, Amazon, and their warehouse WMS. Stockouts and overselling were common.

The Solution

We built a real-time inventory sync engine using n8n that listens for webhooks from all platforms. It instantly updates stock levels across channels whenever a sale occurs.

The Outcome

Reduced manual work to 0 hours. Eliminated overselling incidents completely. Allowed the team to launch on 2 new marketplaces in one month.

Marketing Agency Reporting

The Problem

Account managers were spending the first 3 days of every month compiling manual CSV reports from Facebook Ads, Google Analytics, and CRMs for clients.

The Solution

We automated data extraction into a BigQuery data warehouse and set up dynamic Looker Studio dashboards. We also added an AI agent to write executive summaries.

The Outcome

Reports are now live 24/7. Account managers saved 24 hours/month each, allowing them to manage 30% more clients without burnout.
